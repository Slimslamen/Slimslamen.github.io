Recommended books

Harry Potter

In the new edition of J.K. Rowling's magical successful series about the wizard Harry Potter, the story comes to life again through Jim Kay's fantastic illustrations.

The Lord Of The Rings

'The Fellowship of the Ring,' the first book in J.R.R. Tolkien's 'The Lord of the Rings' trilogy, follows Frodo Baggins and his diverse group of companions.

Game Of Thrones

'A Game of Thrones' by George R.R. Martin is the first book in the 'A Song of Ice and Fire' series. Set in the fictional realms of Westeros and Essos, it follows the Stark family's involvement in the struggle for the Iron Throne.

Dune

Dune, the first novel in Frank Herbert's science fiction series, unfolds on the desert planet Arrakis, the only source of a valuable spice. The story follows Paul Atreides.
